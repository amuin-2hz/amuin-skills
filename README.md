# Amuin · Don't Be a Cog

> Your computer should work for you. Not the other way around.

**Amuin** = "Am I a machine?" — a quiet refusal to be a cog. We share small, practical tools that make your devices faster, your workflow smoother, and your life a little less frustrating.

No jargon. No paid "optimizers." Just things that work, updated as we find better ways.

## Why Amuin?

Every time your C drive fills up and your computer crawls, someone tells you to buy a bigger SSD. Every time a backup eats your disk overnight, the answer is "just get more cloud storage."

These are small problems with free fixes. But the fixes are buried in forums, scattered across outdated tutorials, or written for Linux users who already know the terminal.

Amuin collects and polishes these fixes into **one-click tools anyone can use** — tested on real machines, with safety checks built in.

Saving you from a sluggish computer *is* saving you money.

## How to Use

Drop these skills into your AI agent (like Claude Code). It will follow the scripts to handle things for you — automatically.

No memorizing commands. No scrolling through tutorials. Just say "free up my C drive" and the agent scans, decides, migrates, and verifies.

Every tool also includes manual scripts if you prefer to run things yourself.
